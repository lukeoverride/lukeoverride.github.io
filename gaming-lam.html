Laboratorio di maturità

Università della Svizzera italiana has an agreement with the high school Liceo Lugano 2 for supervising projects of computer science. I have been part of the project for three years.

Naiade, 2020

This videogame is a gamification of the life of Claudia, an italian photographer who suffers from Small fiber peripheral neuropathy. In the first level Claudia is at the hospital, and has to escape from nurses. In the second level she is back at home, and needs to take medicines to be fine. The third level is her photo studio where she will process the film. In the last level, she goes for a trip in the outer space to not suffer from gravity forces. At the end of each level, she meets a Naiad, a water nymph who helps her get through it. The dialogues are in italian. If you want to know more about Claudia's story, please visit her website.
Authors: Mattia Zorzi, Nicholas Cardo, Greta Merlini.
Acknowledgements: We thank Claudia Amatruda for agreeing to be represented as the main character of the videogame and for the valuable information she provided during the project.

Plastic Hunt, 2021

Darwin is the last fish remained in the oceans. His friends and relatives disappeared because of Pietrolio, a bunch of garbage sent by humans. The story of the game is told from the point of view of the fish. The man is a unknown being. The two levels aim to entertain, but also to show what a poor fish and the environment experience because of us. The fishes cannot resolve the situation, we have to do something to help!
Authors: Noah Gagliardi, Ami De Carli.
Acknowledgements: This videogame has been inspired by the event Plastic Hunt, founded by Sofia Bonicalza. We thank her for the time dedicated to the interview and the suggestions in the design of the videogame.
