Instructions:
- Ajla: click left mouse button or tap to run as fast as possible.
- Luminosa: click left mouse button or tap to jump obstacles. You can hit them, but probably you will fall down!
If you are playing from smartphone (even if it is recommended to use a computer), it is advised to: 1) rotate your telephone horizontally; 2) enter full screen mode by pressing the arrows at the bottom-right corner of the game screen after the initial loading has completed.
To restart the game, click twice or simply refresh the page. Enjoy!
